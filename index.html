Music is an incredibly important aspect of Sean's life. She began studying piano at 7-years-old and continues to play today. In addition to piano, she has been heavily involved in choir throughout her life, which led her to pursuing music education in college.

Sean received her Bachelor's in Music Education Magna Cum Laude from The University of Texas at San Antonio in December 2022. In her time there, she was able to study abroad in Italy where she learned Italian culture, history, and language.

Sean has developed an interest in web development and is determined to pursue a career in tech. She is currently studying at Altcademy to become a full-stack web developer. As a life learner, she believes her background in music education will greatly benefit her attitude about learning new skills throughout her career.
